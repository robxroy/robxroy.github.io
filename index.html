Rob X. Roy is a Full Stack Web Development student at the University of Pennsylvania College of Liberal and Professional Studies. Rob has a degree in English and several years of experience as an English teacher.

Through UPenn's web development program, Rob has learned html, css, javascript, jquery, node.js, mysql, express.js, and handlebars. Rob's portfolio demonstrates utilization of several of these coding language and libraries.

Rob wants to use a knowledge of coding to build a career in the field as well as help provide greater access to civic participation and pass along this knowledge to communities who do not have as easy access to the foundations of web development. Rob's hobbies include storytelling, songwriting, citizen activism, and travel.
