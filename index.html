About Me

Rob X. Roy is a Full Stack Web Development student at the University of Pennsylvania College of Liberal and Professional Studies. Rob was born and raised in Northern California's central valley. Rob is excited to discuss history, neighborhood-level diversity-indexes, and cinema. While achieving a degree in English from the University of California -- Davis, Rob was a radio DJ, an ice cream man, and a community activist.

After graduating university, Rob taught English abroad for several years. While in South Korea, Rob met a lady from Philadelphia. They traveled the world together and when returning to live in the states Rob moved to Philadelphia. Rob's hobbies include storytelling, songwriting, and citizen activism.

Rob wants to use a knowledge of coding to build a career in the field as well as help provide greater access to civic participation and pass along this knowledge to communities who do not have as easy access to the foundations of web development.
