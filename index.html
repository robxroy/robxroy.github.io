About Rob

Rob X. Roy is a Full Stack Web Development student at the University of Pennsylvania College of Liberal and Professional Studies. Rob is from Northern California is excited to discuss history, neighborhood-level diversity-indexes, and cinema. While achieving a degree in English from the University of California -- Davis, Rob was a radio DJ, an ice cream man, and a community activist.

Rob has six years of experience teaching English abroad. He recently moved to Philadelphia to live with his wife, who is from the city, and --like Rob -- also taught in South Korea. Rob's hobbies include storytelling, songwriting, citizen activism, and travel (having been to more than 20 countries).

Rob wants to use a knowledge of coding to build a career in the field as well as help provide greater access to civic participation and pass along this knowledge to communities who do not have as easy access to the foundations of web development.
